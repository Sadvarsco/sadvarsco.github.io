MetaMadness Selection Screen

To help see who is and isn't available for hero selection.

How does it work:

  • The first 18 Heroes selected will be considered 'Pre-banned'. After 18 Heroes are selected, they will be grayed out.
  • Every Hero selected after the 'Pre-banned' should be considered "In-Game Hero Picks" for the match. They will turn Red and be unselectable.
  • Hitting the "Hide Banned/Picked" Button will move all 'Pre-banned' and "In-Game Hero Picks" to the bottom of the list.
  • Hitting reset will refresh the page back to a default state.

NOTE: The Heroes are mostly in alphabetical order (mostly grouped by letter). Cho and Gall are currently considered 2 separate Heroes.
